Our Story

Sadhu Baba Foundation was born from a simple yet powerful belief: no one should go to bed hungry. Founded by Sadhu Yadav in the spiritual city of Varanasi, our organization has been dedicated to serving the most vulnerable members of our community.

Every day, we witness the harsh reality of hunger on the streets of Varanasi. From elderly individuals with no family support to homeless people struggling to survive, we see faces that remind us why our work is so crucial.

What started as a small initiative by our founder has grown into a daily operation that feeds over 200 genuine needy people. We don't just provide food; we provide hope, dignity, and the message that someone cares.

Under the leadership of Sadhu Yadav, our foundation continues to expand its reach while maintaining the personal touch and compassion that defines our mission.

Our Mission

To ensure that no person in Varanasi goes to sleep hungry by providing daily nutritious meals to those who need it most, while treating everyone with dignity and respect.

Our Vision

A world where hunger is eliminated and every individual has access to basic nutrition, creating stronger, more compassionate communities.

Our Values

Compassion, dignity, transparency, and unwavering commitment to serving humanity without discrimination of any kind.
